## Intro
The following exercises demonstrate bit manipulation by hand.

## Bit Manipulation by hand

1. 0110 + 0010 = 1000
2. 0011 + 0010 = 0101
3. 0110 - 0011 = 0011
4. 1000 - 0110 = 0010
5. 0011 * 0101 = 1111 
6. 0011 * 0011 = 1001
7. 11001 >> 2 = 0011
8. 1101 ^ 0101 = 1000
9. 0110 + 0110 = 1100
10. 0100 * 0011 = 1100
11. 1101 ^ (~1101) = 1111
12. 1001 & 100 = 1000

## Bitwise notation
1. >> == shift right
2. << == shift left
3. ^ == XOR
4. ~ == NOT
5. & == and 
6. | == or
